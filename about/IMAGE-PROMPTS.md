# Image generation prompts — "Off the Clock" section

Generate these in your image tool of choice (Claude app, DALL·E, Midjourney, etc.),
then save the results in this folder with the filenames below. The site picks them
up automatically.

> Minimal modern editorial illustration, duotone, limited warm palette — deep
> charcoal background #1C1518, copper/amber #C8825A, champagne gold #D9B36C, soft
> cream #ECE0D6. Clean confident shapes, subtle film grain, soft directional
> light, no text, no watermark, 3:2 landscape.
